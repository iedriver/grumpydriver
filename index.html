" Grumpy Driver "

I have been a truck driver for fifteen years and I know that in my work I need information from where I need to go.

Transporting goods from place to place from customer to customer is our main occupation at work.

After many years of observation, I decided to take a step forward and created a website for suppliers like me.

I will write only a few of the benefits it offers: reduction of driver stress, significant reduction of delivery time, reduction of the risk of an accident associated with excessive attachment of the driver to searching for a delivery point and not on the basis of road traffic.

Also, the employer should benefit because using the help offered by me will give him a less stressed employee, less money spent on fuel, reducing the risk of an accident reduces the risk of losing an employee, etc.

The website is built in a very simple and understandable for everyone.

Go to the search tab and enter your client's account number there.

If it is not in the database, you can add information yourself using the bookmark, add a new customer.

At the bottom of the page is an email to me if you have any suggestions for changes in appearance or suggestions for adding something new.

I wish you successful use of my website and safe work.

And always remember "Safety First & Teamwork"
